Problem Statement: Organizing Digital Communication

Our users are having trouble keeping track of and responding to important messages because they are spread out across multiple messaging services. Our solution should aim to provide a way to organize and sort all messages from one place.

My group and I worked together to create an affinity diagram to come up with ideas for our program that will help people organize their messages and important information into one place.
